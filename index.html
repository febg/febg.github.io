Legacy

The Minichiello legacy is one of innovation, history, and legendary star power. Originating from Civitanova del Sanni in Italy, founder Paul Minichiello opened his shop in 1964 in the shadow of The Lions, the noblest of the North Shore mountains at the edge of Vancouver, BC, Canada. The 1970s were a blur of creating for celebrities, sports stars, and millionaires. Cattivo Ragazzo is the legacy of Paul’s craftsmanship, quality, self-assurance, and success. Dino Minichiello represents the current generation and perpetuates a legacy which delivers a truly bespoke solution to the successful self-made man.

Masterpieces

Capo

Enter the world of Cattivo Ragazzo with a choice of the first tier finery. A beautiful selection of fabrics awaits your decision and creates the base to which to add your choice of Bemberg or Emerzine lining, printed with a range of themes. Finish your Capo style with a unique moulded-resin Barretto set and perfect buttons.

Capo Famiglia

Join the Cattivo Ragazzo family with the finest suit imaginable. Enjoy the services of a personal stylist who will guide the process from concept to completion. Choose from the speciality fabrics, exquisite hand-painted silk-linings with unique themes by established artists, and explore the selection of VIP embellishments and exclusive buttons. The finishing touch is the stylist-assisted designing of a handcrafted Barretto set incorporating precious metals and gemstones.

Capo Bastone

Chose the second tier of Cattivo Ragazzo finery which encompasses all the style trim of the Capo with an expanded selection of speciality fabrics. Choose from a selection of limited edition themes by established artists for the Bemberg or Emerzine linings. Step-up from moulded-resin to a stone or polished metal Barretto set and create edgy sophistication with polished bone and horn buttons.

About

Bespoke suiting is a traditional experience and while the Minichiello family has been delivering this for decades, Cattivo Ragazzo is serving it with a twist. Offering sophisticated and modern men’s wear that blends elements of high-end fashion with an edgy urban aesthetic, the Vancouver atelier is creating a movement that speaks to a new generation of driven, self-made men who desire to make bold and edgy lifestyle statements with both their lives and actions.
